It IS That Easy

Bog-standard pumps require blood, sweat, and tears before you can start pumping. NFLATER™ lets you breeze through it all like nothing.

You have the SOS/lightning switch to the left and the unit switch to the right, enabling you to choose between 4 measurement units (PSI, BAR, KPA, and Kg/cm²). The plus button atop increases the pressure, the minus button below decreases it.

Easy stuff, right? Also, the screen above the controls highlights the pressure unit that you’ve selected and displays the current pressure.

Does that mean no guesswork? You damn right it does. And, when the tire pressure reaches the preset value, NFLATER™ automatically turns off, conserving battery life and keeping things safe and quick.

FAQ
What is this thing made of?

NFLATER™ is made of high-strength ABS plastic and sports a high-tensile aluminum shell. The plastic makes the pump lightweight but durable, whereas the metal shell protects it from shock damage.

Does this device measure the tire pressure before inflating?

Yes! NFLATER™ shows you how much pressure there currently is inside the tire and automatically shuts off when the tire pressure reaches the preset value.

What’s the maximum pressure this air compressor can reach?

NFLATER™ can inflate the tire up to 120 PSI, so it should easily cover most of your ‘inflation’ needs.

How long does it take to recharge this car pump?

NFLATER™ goes from 0% to 100% in ~3 hours, which is super quick considering its high-capacity 6,000 mAh battery. That charge should last you up to 90 minutes of non-stop pumping.

Does it get hot and is it very loud?

No! Thanks to the built-in fan and the mesh on the left side, NFLATER™ dissipates heat and keeps its cool easily. And it is no louder than your typical pump!
